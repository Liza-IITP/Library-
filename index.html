Grimoires of Elements

The Grimoires of Elements are a collection of ancient tomes that hold the secrets of elemental magic. Each grimoire is dedicated to a specific element, such as fire, water, earth, or air, and contains spells, rituals, and knowledge related to that element. These grimoires are highly sought after by wizards and witches for their powerful enchantments and insights into the elemental forces of nature.
